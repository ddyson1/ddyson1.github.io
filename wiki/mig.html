This article is about the language-learning application. For the Russian aircraft design bureau, see Mikoyan.
MiG
DeveloperDevin Dyson
Written inSwift
PlatformiOS
TypeLanguage learning · vocabulary trainer
Release2026 (planned)

MiG is a native iOS application by Devin Dyson that trains vocabulary for learners of Russian through speed-reading drills built for rapid word recognition.[1] The app ships with more than 1,500 curated words spanning CEFR levels A1 to B1, and is planned for release on the App Store in 2026.[1]

The project grew out of Dyson's own study of Russian, one of the languages he is learning alongside his native English and basic conversational Mandarin.[1]

worldView

From devindyson.com, the free personal encyclopedia
Not to be confused with world view, one's fundamental cognitive orientation.
worldView
TypeMorning dashboard
AuthorDevin Dyson
Written inPython
StatusPrivate repository

worldView is a morning dashboard by Devin Dyson that distills high-level developments from around the globe — including financial markets and geopolitical events — into a single daily view.[1]

The project is an outgrowth of Dyson's undergraduate study of international business at Ithaca College, and an early expression of the interest in global systems that later informed his work at the MIT Media Lab's Digital Currency Initiative. Originally published on GitHub, it is now maintained in a private repository.[1]
